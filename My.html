The beginning

One day I was scrolling through youtube and I found a cool video showing some great moves and tricks.
I got surprised by that and watched it about 10 times till the next day.
So i began watching such videos from the reccomendations and a few days later i learned that it's a sport called parkour.
I then started watching tutorials for some moves and practiced them on two nearby placed beds.
I got more and more involved in it and continued such practice. And this was how I got into parkour.
